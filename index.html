Recent projects

On the side, I'm interested in adding dplyr-like functionality to D3 (and Observable). I'm also trying be a better data modeler and to contribute to the Stan project. But these are some things further along than that.

Improving D3-color and adding new D3 color modules

I've been added as a contributor to D3.js to add new color modules, based on newer percentually uniform color spaces not yet available in D3's color library. A beta of the first module, d3-hsluv, is on Github. I'm workign on a second module, CIECAM16, with Connor Gramazio.

Maps + Chartbuilder

I built a way to render US, world, county and district choropleth and cartogram maps into Chartbuilder working in partnership with Quartz.

NBC News Election Results

I oversaw visualization design and development for the NBC News's election results pages. We presented national Presidential, House, Senate and governors races. We build a server-side implementation of D3 for Node and Slackbots to optimize the process.

D3.js on TV (election night)

NBC News and MNSBC used D3.js to render maps on election night. I created an initial prototype to spur the idea for the product and managed a contractor who helped us build the app that ran election night.A few screengrabs here.
